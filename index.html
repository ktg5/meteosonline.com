What is Meteos Online?

Meteos Online (メテオスオンライン) was a online Japanese PC game that ran from 2006 to 2007 pulished by Hangame and developed by Q Entertainment.

Meteos Online was based off of Q Entertainment's NDS title, Meteos.

The online game was free-to-play for anyone but allowed users to pay for in-game points to use in-game to collect avatar clothing, planets, or items. The game ran on PCs from Win98 to WinXP and possibly up.

Q Entertainment would shut down the Meteos Online servers on October 31, 2007 at 5:00 PM JST.

Game status?

Since Meteos Online's shut down, there has been no archived download for the client used to connect to the service.

Our goal since June 2022 is attempting to find and archive this client and possibly the servers the client would connect to, we'll be posting as many updates we can to our Discord about anything related to our search.

What can I do to help?

Glad you asked! If you believe you might have a computer or hard drive that might have played or installed Meteos Online from the time, check to see if the folder location C:\Programs Files\QOnline\MeteosOnline is on your drive and then get into contact with the staff on our Discord

Otherwise, spread the word over the internet! Reddit, YouTube, Twitter, what have you.
